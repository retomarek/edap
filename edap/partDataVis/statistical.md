# Statistical Plots

Statistical visualizations help to understand the distribution of values, identify outliers, and explore relationships between variables.

## Recipes

- **Boxplot** — Compare distributions across categories (e.g., flats, seasons)
- **Density Plot** — Visualize the probability density of a variable
- **Density Plot by Season** — Compare distributions across heating/cooling seasons using pyedautils
- **Cross-Correlation** — Analyze the time-lagged relationship between two variables
