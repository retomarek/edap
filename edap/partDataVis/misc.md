# Miscellaneous Visualizations

Additional visualization recipes that do not fit neatly into the other categories but are commonly used in energy data analysis.

## Recipes

- **Energy Signature** — Plot heating/cooling demand against outdoor temperature
- **Energy Signature PES** — Energy signature following the PES method (Eriksson 2020)
- **Electricity Household** — Visualize household electricity consumption patterns
- **Plotly Multiple Y-Axes** — Combine multiple variables with different units on one chart
