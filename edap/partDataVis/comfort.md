# Comfort Diagrams

Comfort diagrams visualize indoor climate conditions and assess whether they meet comfort standards. These plots are essential for building performance evaluation.

## Recipes

- **Temperature vs. Humidity** — Scatter plot of indoor temperature and humidity with comfort zone overlay
- **Mollier h-x Diagram** — Psychrometric chart showing the relationship between temperature, humidity, and enthalpy
- **SIA 180 Adaptive Comfort** — Evaluate indoor temperatures against the SIA 180:2014 adaptive comfort model
