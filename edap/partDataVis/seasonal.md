# Seasonal Visualizations

Seasonal visualizations overlay or compare data across different time periods (e.g., years, months) to reveal recurring patterns and changes over time.

## Recipes

- **Overlapping Years** — Overlay multiple years on a single axis to compare seasonal patterns
- **Seasonal Miniplots** — Small multiples showing each year or month side by side
- **Polar Chart** — Circular visualization of seasonal data (e.g., heating demand by month)
- **Before/After Comparison** — Compare a variable before and after an intervention or event
